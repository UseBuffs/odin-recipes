Pizza

How to make tasty pizza!

You will need:

    Ingredients
  • 300g flour
  • 1 tsp yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 200ml water

    • Steps:

  1. Bring Ingredients together in a mixer
  2. Mix for 5-10min until dough is elastic
  3. Rest dough for 30min
  4. Make dough-balls and then wait till they have doubled in size
  5. Pizza time!
